<h2><a class="anchor" id="project-overview"></a>📌 Project Overview</h2>
Exploratory and statistical analysis of vendor sales, pricing efficiency, and inventory turnover — with hypothesis testing to validate profitability variance across vendor segments.

🔗 Tableau Dashboard:
https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/shrey.parekh/viz/VendorPerformanceAnalysis_17747647991500/VendorPerformanceDashboard

🔍 Key Insights
Top 10 vendors contribute ~65% of total purchase — indicating supplier concentration risk
Significant variation in profit margins across vendors suggests pricing inefficiencies
Low-performing vendors identified based on stock turnover and sales contribution
Inventory-heavy vendors show lower profitability, highlighting working capital concerns

🛠️ Tech Stack
Python (Pandas, NumPy)
SQL (Data extraction & transformation)
Tableau (Dashboard & visualization)
Statistical Analysis (Hypothesis Testing)
