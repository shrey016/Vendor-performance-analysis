<h2 id="project-overview">📌 Project Overview</h2>

Exploratory and statistical analysis of vendor sales, pricing efficiency, and inventory turnover — with hypothesis testing to validate profitability variance across vendor segments.

<br>

🔗 <b>Tableau Dashboard:</b> <a href="https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/shrey.parekh/viz/VendorPerformanceAnalysis_17747647991500/VendorPerformanceDashboard" target="_blank">
Vendor Performance Dashboard </a>

<br><br>

🔍 <b>Key Insights:</b>

<ul>
  <li>Top 10 vendors contribute ~65% of total purchase, indicating supplier concentration risk</li>
  <li>Significant variation in profit margins across vendors suggests pricing inefficiencies</li>
  <li>Low-performing vendors identified based on stock turnover and sales contribution</li>
  <li>Inventory-heavy vendors show lower profitability, highlighting working capital concerns</li>
</ul>

🛠️ <b>Tech Stack:</b>

<ul>
  <li>Python (Pandas, NumPy)</li>
  <li>SQL (Data extraction & transformation)</li>
  <li>Tableau (Dashboard & visualization)</li>
  <li>Statistical Analysis (Hypothesis Testing)</li>
</ul>
