<h2><a class="anchor" id="project-overview"></a>📌 Project Overview</h2>
Exploratory and statistical analysis of vendor sales, pricing efficiency, and inventory turnover — with hypothesis testing to validate profitability variance across vendor segments.<br>

🔗 Tableau Dashboard:

https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/shrey.parekh/viz/VendorPerformanceAnalysis_17747647991500/VendorPerformanceDashboard
<br>
🔍 Key Insights:

Top 10 vendors contribute ~65% of total purchase — indicating supplier concentration risk<br>
Significant variation in profit margins across vendors suggests pricing inefficiencies<br>
Low-performing vendors identified based on stock turnover and sales contribution<br>
Inventory-heavy vendors show lower profitability, highlighting working capital concerns<br>

🛠️ Tech Stack:

Python (Pandas, NumPy)
SQL (Data extraction & transformation)
Tableau (Dashboard & visualization)
Statistical Analysis (Hypothesis Testing)
